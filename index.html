Top three activities to do at Eskişehir

Explore Odunpazarı

Walk through the historic Odunpazarı district, famous for its colorful Ottoman-era houses and charming streets.

Enjoy a Gondola Ride on the Porsuk River

Experience Eskişehir’s Venice-like atmosphere with a relaxing gondola or boat ride along the Porsuk River

Visit Sazova Science, Culture, and Art Park

Discover a fairytale-like castle, explore the science museum, and enjoy the aquarium at Sazova Park
